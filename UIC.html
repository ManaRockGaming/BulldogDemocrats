The United Independence Caucus, commonly also known as the United Independence Coalition, is a grand coalition of liberals, socialists, and alienated conservatives who desire to build a New Carolina. We believe that by uniting the disenfranchised people of South Carolina we can unite to oppose the Republican Party's control of every segment of the State Government since 1998.

The College Democrats at The Citadel

Interested in meeting with fellow cadets/students/alumni with a similar viewpoint, or to hear a new perspective? Want to meet and build connections with local elected officials and policymakers? Want to make a positive difference? All are invited to participate and join The College Democrats at The Citadel, a chance to build a new Carolina.

The College Democrats at The Citadel is a student-led organization supported by the Bulldog Democrats, an alumni initiative. The Bulldog Democrats is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit under the law of South Carolina, with Articles of Incorporation filed accordinly as a DBA for the United Independence Coalition.
