"The Ground Floor Of A New Carolina"

The Citadel College Democrats (CCD) Society is a mighty bastion dedicated to promoting democratic values on the traditionally conservative campus. No group has more passion or greater perseverance as we work tirelessly to advocate for social justice and civic engagement, organizing events and fostering dialogue. We invite you to join us in this great endeavor to build the ground floor for a new and brighter Carolina.
