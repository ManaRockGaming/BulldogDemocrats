Financials - General Ledger

We believe that one of the most important values in politics is transparency. That's why we openly share our financial records and investment choices on this website. The Bulldog Democrats should lead by example, being honest and open about where your money goes, how it's managed, and what it supports.

Revenue from investments plays a crucial role alongside donations in helping to smooth funding availability for events and outreach. Honor, Duty, and Respect. All of our investments are made with consideration of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) in mind. This ensures that we're not only making smart financial choices, but also aligning our finances with our values. Supporting companies that demonstrate responsibility for our planet, fair labor practices, ethical leadership, and good governance.
