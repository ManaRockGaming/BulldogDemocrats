The College socialists society at The Citadel

Is a shadowy cabal of socialists haunting The Citadel's conservative order? Not to worry, we are only here to help build a New Carolina. We are the student and alumni socialists of The Citadel, the Military College of South Carolina. Interested in joining the fight for political, social, and economic freedom why not join us?

The College socialists society is open to all types of socialists, progressives, communists, anarchists, & leftists. Due to the political climate at The Citadel, many of our supporters are also members of The Citadel College Democrat Society, our sister organization.

Who Is A Bulldog Socialist?

Anyone who fights for justice and equality with a greater world in their hearts, is a Bulldog Socialist. The Bulldog Socialist is an alumni, faculty, and friends association designed to build connections. If it was not for the great support of our Bulldog Socialists, the CSS would not be the society it is today.
