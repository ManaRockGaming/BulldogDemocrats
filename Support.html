SUPPORT FINANCIALLY**

Monetary donations are used to spread democratic values at The Citadel, through funding CDS events and functions. By donating to the CDS, not only are you putting your money into a good cause, but directly investing in the future democratic leaders of America. For those unable to make a monetary donation, following us on social media goes a long way toward spreading democratic values.


Bulldog Democrats is organized as a 501(c)(4) nonprofit under the laws of South Carolina, with Articles of Incorporation filed accordingly as a DBA. Donations to the Bulldog Democrats cannot be deducted due to IRS Regulation 501(c)(4).


All Bulldog Democrats financials are available online on the "Financials" Page
